Emo-StarGAN: Semi-Supervised, Any-to-Many Non-Parallel Emotion Preserving Voice Conversion
==========================================================================================

1. There are 20 folders, having naming convention: <source_emotion>_<folder_id>

2. Each folder has 3 files:
	i) source.wav: the stimuli or the original file, which is used for conversion. The emotion of the source file is depicted by the folder name.
	ii) baseline.wav: the conversion produced by the baseline model.
	iii) emo-stargan.wav: the conversion produced by our method (Emo-StarGAN).
